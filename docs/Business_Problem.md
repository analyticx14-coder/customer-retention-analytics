# Business Problem

## Background

Customer retention is one of the most important challenges for modern businesses. Acquiring a new customer is often more expensive than retaining an existing one.

The company has observed that a significant number of customers are leaving the platform. Management wants to understand the reasons behind customer churn and identify opportunities to improve customer retention.

## Project Goal

The goal of this project is to analyze customer behavior, identify the key drivers of churn, and provide data-driven recommendations that help reduce customer attrition.

## Business Objectives

- Measure customer churn rate.
- Identify customer segments with high churn.
- Analyze behavioral patterns associated with churn.
- Discover factors influencing customer retention.
- Build an interactive dashboard for business stakeholders.
